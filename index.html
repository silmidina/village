Our Food Menu

  • Ayam Betutu

    Chicken seasoned with Balinese spices and wrapped in banana leaves before being grilled until cooked. This dish has a strong and fragrant taste, and is often served at special events in Bali.

  • Tea or Coffee

    A traditional Balinese drink consisting of colored agar-agar, sweet syrup, and coconut milk, refreshing and colorful.
